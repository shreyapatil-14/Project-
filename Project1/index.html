Fish are a very high source of protein and have great nutritional value.

Fish production was initially dependent on fish capturing However most of the captured fish were used for industrial purposes and were hardly consumed by man.

An alternative method to increase fish production was devised that includes farming and husbandry of economically important aquatic organisms.

This is known as FISH FARMING.

In this method, commercial breeding and raising of fish is done in enclosures or in fish tanks.Fish farming is the fastest growing area of animal food production.


Fish husbandry is done in two different environments.
1) Freshwater.
2) Marine.
